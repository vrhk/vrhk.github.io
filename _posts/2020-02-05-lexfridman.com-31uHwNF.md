### Jim Keller: Moore's Law, Microprocessors, Abstractions, and First Principles | MIT | Artificial Intelligence Podcast

Jim Keller is a legendary microprocessor engineer, having worked at AMD, Apple, Tesla, and now Intel. He’s known for his work on the AMD K7, K8, K12 and Zen microarchitectures, Apple A4, A5 processors, and co-author of the specifications for the x86-64 instruction set and HyperTransport interconnect.
