### Trusted News - This plugin analyses the quality of news articles | Product Hunt

Trusted News uses AI to assist newsreaders in evaluating the quality of the online content they read. In its first release, it scores the objectivity for a selected article, testing whether it is written from a neutral perspective as oppose to a personal one.
