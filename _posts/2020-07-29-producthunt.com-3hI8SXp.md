### FORM Smart Swim Goggles - See GPS metrics and heart rate in real time in open water | Product Hunt

FORM launched a firmware update which enables swimmers to view GPS performance metrics like distance, pace, heart rate, and more, in real-time while swimming outdoors by connecting the FORM goggles to their compatible Garmin® smartwatch or Apple Watch.
