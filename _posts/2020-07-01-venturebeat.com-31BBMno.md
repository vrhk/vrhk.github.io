### Learn how to accelerate your business using automation and AI technology: Transform 2020

No one expected the jolt the Covid-19 pandemic would bring to business. If companies were already investing in automation and AI technologies before March 2020, they have only accelerated those investments since. With leaders looking for ways to avoid human contact, machines, software and new processes that avoid those humans are even more imperative.
