### OpenAI Scholars Spring 2020

We are now accepting applications for our third class of OpenAI Scholars, a 4 month full-time program where we provide stipends and mentorship to 8 individuals from underrepresented groups [<https://www.nsf.gov/statistics/2017/nsf17310/digest/introduction/>] to study deep learning and produce open-source a project. The second class of Scholars recently released their projects [/blog/openai-scholars-class-of-19/] and presented their work at the 2019 Scholars Demo Day. [<https://www.youtube.com/watch>
